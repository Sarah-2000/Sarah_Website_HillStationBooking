MADPACKERS HOSTEL:


Madpackers Hostel Manali in Manali provides adults only accommodations with a shared lounge, a garden and a terrace. 
The property is located 2.4 km from Hidimba Devi Temple, a 3-minute walk from Manu Temple and 1.4 km from Circuit House. 
The property provides a tour desk, ticket service and currency exchange for guests.
Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, rooms at the hostel also have free WiFi, while selected rooms here will provide you with a balcony.
Tibetan Monastery is 2.9 km from Madpackers Hostel Manali. 
The nearest airport is Kullu–Manali Airport, 53.1 km from the accommodation.
Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.7 for a two-person trip.
