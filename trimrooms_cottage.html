TRIMROOMS COTTAGE:



Located in Manali, 6.4 km from Hidimba Devi Temple, Trimrooms Cottage Manali provides accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, a shared lounge and a garden. 
Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. 
The property has a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.
All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. 
Free WiFi is available to all guests, while selected rooms here will provide you with a patio. 
The rooms will provide guests with a desk and an electric tea pot.
Trimrooms Cottage Manali offers a continental or buffet breakfast.
The area is popular for hiking, and car rental is available at the accommodation.
Tibetan Monastery is 4.2 km from Trimrooms Cottage Manali, while Circuit House is 5.3 km away. 
The nearest airport is Kullu–Manali, 49.9 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.3 for a two-person trip.
